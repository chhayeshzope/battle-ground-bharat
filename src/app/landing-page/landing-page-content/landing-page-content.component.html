Everything you need to know about BattleGrounds Bharat.

BattleGrounds Bharat distinguishes itself with unique Indian-inspired maps, dynamic weather systems, cultural festival events, and vehicles that reflect India's identity. We aim to create a rich, immersive experience rooted in Indian culture.

What makes BattleGrounds Bharat different from other battle royales?

Dynamic festivals introduce temporary gameplay modifiers. For example, during Holi, temporary color-smoke grenades might be available, offering unique tactical advantages. Diwali could bring firework distractions, affecting visibility and sound cues.

How do dynamic festivals impact gameplay?

Players will traverse the maps using iconic Indian vehicles like auto-rickshaws, Royal Enfield-style motorcycles, fishing boats, and even local trains.
